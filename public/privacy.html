Vaani Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Vaani ("we", "our", "us") provides a WhatsApp AI Sales Bot for Shopify stores. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect data.

2. Data We Collect

  • Shopify store domain and access token (to fetch products and orders)
  • Customer WhatsApp phone numbers (to send and receive messages)
  • Conversation history (to provide contextual AI responses)
  • Cart data (to assist with purchases)

3. How We Use Data

  • To provide AI-powered WhatsApp sales assistance
  • To fetch product and order information from your Shopify store
  • To send automated follow-up messages for abandoned carts
  • To improve our AI responses

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Anthropic (AI processing) — subject to their privacy policy
  • Meta/WhatsApp (message delivery) — subject to their privacy policy
  • Railway (hosting) — subject to their privacy policy

5. Data Retention

Conversation history is retained for 90 days. Store data is deleted within 30 days of uninstalling the app.

6. GDPR Compliance

Store owners can request deletion of all data by emailing us. We provide data export on request.
