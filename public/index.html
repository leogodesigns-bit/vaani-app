How it works

Three steps from
chat to checkout.

Vaani handles the entire sales conversation — so your team doesn't have to.

01

Customer says hello

A WhatsApp link on your website or Instagram triggers a personalised greeting from Vaani — in your brand's voice.

02

Vaani shows & sells

Products are shown with images, prices, and shortlisting — all inside the chat. Customers browse without leaving WhatsApp.

03

Order confirmed

Vaani creates a Shopify draft order and sends a payment link. Your team gets notified. Done.
