How we count chats

One unique customer / day = one chat.

Whether they send 2 messages or 20 in a day, it's still one chat. Conversations across multiple days from the same number count as separate chats. Honest, predictable billing — no message-counting anxiety.

Run out? Just top up

₹500 = 250 chats, anytime.

Top-ups stack on top of your monthly cap and stay valid for 3 months. Used oldest-first. No re-signup, no recontract — just keep selling.

Cancel anytime
