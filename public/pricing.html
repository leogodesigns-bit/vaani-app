How chats are counted

Simple. No hidden math.

One conversation

One unique customer per day = one chat. Multiple messages with the same customer in 24 hours still count as one.

Broadcast messages

Broadcast messages are charged separately at Meta rates — approximately ₹1.20 per message (includes template approval). You only pay for what you send.
