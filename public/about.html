Our story

Vaani came out of a simple frustration — we kept building Shopify stores for great brands, and watched them lose sales because WhatsApp was a mess of manual replies.

So we built the tool we always wished existed. Vaani is a Leogo Designs product — made in Pune, built for India. Ikaa was our first brand to go live with it.

Why we built Vaani

Your customers are
on WhatsApp right now.

Every brand we worked with had the same story — WhatsApp flooded with product questions. "What sizes do you have?" "Can you send me photos?" "How do I pay?" Hours of manual replies, every day.

The tools that existed were either built for global markets — expensive, complex, English-only — or too basic to handle a real sales conversation. Nothing was built for how Indian brands actually sell.

Vaani is our answer. A WhatsApp sales assistant that's always on — multilingual, Shopify-native, and designed around how your customers actually shop. Ikaa's Jhilmil bot was our first proof it works.

We spent years helping brands look incredible online. Vaani is about making sure they actually sell — 24/7, not just when someone's available to reply.

Shweta · Founder, Leogo Designs & Vaani
