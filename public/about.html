About Vaani

Built by people who've watched great brands lose sales to unanswered WhatsApps.

We didn't build Vaani because AI was trending. We built it because we'd spent six years building beautiful Shopify stores for Indian brands — and kept watching the same problem kill their sales.

Why we built this

Every brand we worked with had the same story. Beautiful store. Great products. And a WhatsApp number that someone had to babysit all day.

Customers would message at 11pm and get a reply the next morning — if they were lucky. They'd ask the same question three different ways because they weren't sure anyone was listening.

The tools that existed were built for global markets. Expensive. Complex. English-only. Nothing was designed for how Indian brands actually sell — in Hindi, in voice notes, in the middle of the night.

So we built Vaani.

We spent years helping brands look incredible online. Vaani is about making sure they actually sell — 24/7, not just when someone's available to reply.
Shweta · Founder, Leogo Designs & Vaani
